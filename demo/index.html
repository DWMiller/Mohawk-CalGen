Mohawk College Calendar Generator

This Calendar Generator is a tool designed to read your Mohawk College timetable and generate a .ics calendar file, allowing you to easily import your class schedule into a variety of calendar applications.

Step 1 Create a Bookmark

Depending on your browser, this may be accomplished several ways.

  • 'Drag' the link to your bookmark bar.
  • Right-click the link and select 'Bookmark This Link'
  • More Help
Bookmark This

Note: This is a special type of bookmark called a 'bookmarklet', rather than acting as a link to a webpage, it will perform an action when clicked.

Step 2 Your Mohawk Timetable

Find your Mohawk College timetable, follow the directions below to locate it. Note: This specific timetable must be used, any other location will not work.

  1. Navigate to mocomotion and login
  2. Go to the 'My Student Account' tab
  3. Find and select the link to view your timetable, located in the left column, shown below.
  4. Select your current or upcoming term and you will be taken to your timetable

Step 3 Use the Bookmarklet

While on this page, with your timetable visible, simply click the bookmark. This will trigger a popup (You must not have popups blocked) and a download of the .ics calendar file.

Step 4 Use the .ics File

That's all. You now have a .ics file, usable by most major calendar applications.
Turorials on how to use your calendar applications will not be provided here.
